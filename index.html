What VerveMD Does

The one line

We get AI-invisible clinics visible, and we make sure every inquiry that starts coming in actually converts.

VerveMD is a marketing agency that works specifically with longevity, concierge, and aesthetic medicine practices. We solve two problems.

Problem 1  ·  AI Visibility
When patients ask ChatGPT or Perplexity "who's the best longevity doctor in Austin," three practices show up. Everyone else is invisible. We fix that.
Problem 2  ·  Inquiry Capture
Inquiries come in after hours, nobody answers, leads go cold. We set up AI that answers, qualifies, and books, even at midnight.
The Offer
Free AI Visibility Audit. No pitch. A real look at where the practice stands in AI search. That's what you're booking.
Your Job on the Call
You're not selling. You're booking the audit, or getting the URL and city so Chris can run it. No pricing, no demos.

Your Script

Before you dial

Practice name, doctor's name if you have it, specialty, city. That's it. The script works cold.

Step 1 · Getting Past the Front Desk When the front desk answers
You
"Hey, how are you doing today?"
Note
Wait. Be warm. One second of real conversation matters.
You
"I'm with Chris Cook at VerveMD. We work with longevity and concierge practices on AI search visibility. Who handles marketing for the practice?"
Them
[They give you a name or transfer you, or say "that's the doctor" / "that's Dr. ___"]
You
"Great, is [name] free for a minute?"
Note
If unavailable: "No problem, what's the best time to reach them?" Note it down. Don't leave a generic voicemail unless you have to.
Step 2 · The Main Call (Decision Maker) When you reach the decision maker
You
"Hey [name], got a quick second?"
Note
Wait. Let them answer first.
You
"I'm with VerveMD, we work with longevity and concierge practices. Quick question. Does your practice show up on ChatGPT or Perplexity when patients search [their specialty] in [their city]?"
Note
Most will say no or "no idea." That's the door. Stay quiet after you ask. Let it land.
Them
"No, I'm not sure." / "I don't think so." / "What do you mean?"
You
"That's why I'm calling. Three practices end up dominating each market in AI search, and it shifts fast. Chris runs a free AI Visibility Audit. One day, no pitch. Worth a look?"
Them
[If yes] "Sure, okay." / "What do you need?"
You
"Perfect. Just need your website URL, primary city, and best email. Chris will run it and send it over."
Note
URL, city, email. That's a win. Log it.
Voicemail Script Only after two tries with no live answer
You
"Hi, this is Samalee with VerveMD on behalf of Chris Cook. We help longevity and concierge practices show up on ChatGPT and Perplexity. Free audit, no obligation. Reach me at [your number] or chris@vervemd.com. Thanks."
Note
Under 25 seconds. Don't sound like you're reading. Voicemails rarely convert.
05

Objections and Responses

Remember

You're not selling, you're offering a free audit. Remind them. Keep the door open. Don't argue.

They say "We already have a marketing company."
"Good. This is different, it's about AI search, separate from SEO and paid ads. Most agencies aren't tracking it yet. The audit just shows what's there."
Why it works: doesn't attack their agency, fills a gap they don't know exists.
They say "Not interested."
"Understood. Is it timing, or just not on the radar?" [If timing] "When's better?" [If not a priority] "Got it, thanks for your time."
Why it works: separates timing from a real no. Most "not interested" is really "not right now."
They say "Can you just send me some information?"
"I'd rather send the actual audit. Specific to your practice, more useful than a brochure. Chris just needs your website URL and primary city. Can I grab those?"
Why it works: redirects to the ask. Personalized info is harder to ignore.
They say "We're not looking to spend more on marketing."
"The audit is free. No obligation, no pitch. Just shows where you stand in AI search. Worth a look?"
Why it works: removes the cost objection by leading with free.
They say "We're fully booked already."
"That's great, so it's about keeping it consistent, right? AI search is where patients without a referral start. The audit shows what's happening there. Free."
Why it works: reframes from new-patient gain to keeping the funnel consistent.
They say "Who are you again?" / "What company is this?"
"VerveMD. We work with longevity and concierge practices on AI search visibility, making sure they show up on ChatGPT and Perplexity in their market."
Why it works: clean re-orient, no defensiveness.
They say "I need to talk to the doctor / owner first."
"Completely understand. Best way to get a note in front of them? Call back at a specific time, or send a summary to an email?"
Why it works: gets a callback time or email instead of a dead end.

What to Log After Each Call

After every call, even a hang-up, log these five things so Chris can track and follow up.

  1. Practice name and phone number
    As it appears on the website or how they answer the phone.
  2. Who you spoke to (or reached)
    Name and role: "front desk," "office manager Jane," "Dr. Patel directly."
  3. Outcome
    One of: Audit requested (WIN, get website + email), Call back scheduled, Not interested, No answer, Left voicemail, Gatekeeper only.
  4. If audit requested: website URL, city, best email
    All three before you hang up. Chris needs them to run the audit.
  5. Any notes on objections or tone
    "Friendly but busy," "call back Friday," "already has an SEO agency," "deferred to doctor." Anything useful for follow-up.
07

Rules to Remember

Always Lead with the free audit. Never a sales call.
Your CTA is always: "Chris does a free audit, no obligation." Never "can I schedule a pitch call."
Always Ask the qualifying question every time.
"Does your practice show up on ChatGPT or Perplexity?" This is the hook. Ask it every call. Most won't know, and that opens the door.
Never Quote pricing.
If they ask: "The audit is free. If it shows something worth addressing, Chris will walk through it then. No pitch before the audit."
Never Try to explain the full service.
Skip AEO, SEO, and the inquiry system. "AI search visibility" is enough. The simpler it sounds, the better.
Never Argue or push hard on a firm no.
Two nos and you move on. Graceful exit keeps the door open for next time.
The only metric that matters

Audits requested per day. Two or three is a great day. Volume matters, 20 to 30 dials a day once you're in a rhythm.
